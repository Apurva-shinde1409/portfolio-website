About Me

I am a data science enthusiast who believes that data has the power to unlock insights shaping industries, communities, and the future. I am motivated by transforming raw, complex data into meaningful and actionable knowledge that drives informed decision-making.

Most of my time is spent working with data—exploring, cleaning, modeling, and uncovering hidden patterns. My technical skill set spans data analysis, data visualization, and machine learning, with hands-on experience in big data, data lakes, and data warehousing technologies. I enjoy building end-to-end data science solutions, from data ingestion to predictive modeling and analytics.

I continuously upskill in machine learning and AI trends, enjoy automating workflows, optimizing processes, and building scalable solutions. Being part of data science communities and attending tech meetups allows me to stay connected, learn collaboratively, and apply practical insights.

I am passionate about leveraging data science for social good, particularly in climate change and sustainability. I actively work on projects to contribute positively to society while staying updated on environmental research and solutions.
